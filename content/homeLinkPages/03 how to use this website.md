## How to use this website

This website is a small reading space. You can start from the home page, follow the links at the bottom, and move through the pages in the order that feels natural to you.

Each page is written to stand on its own. If you are new here, begin with the first page that catches your attention rather than trying to read everything from the beginning.

Use the home button in the header whenever you want to return to the starting point. Some pages may also include headings, notes, links, audio, or video. When they do, treat them as guides rather than requirements.

If a page mentions another topic, open it when you need more context. Otherwise, keep reading. The site is meant to support slow browsing, rereading, and following your own path.

There is no account to make and no special setup. Read what is useful, leave what is not, and come back later when you want to continue.
